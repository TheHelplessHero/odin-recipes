Lasagna Recipe

Description

A simple lasagna recipe that will save you lots of time and satisfy everyone at the dinner table.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of Meat:
    • Beef
    • Turkey
    • Ham
  • Spaghetti Sauce: Use store-bought, homemade makes this a 'not-so-simple' recipe.
  • Cheeses:
    • Cottage Cheese
    • Mozzarella
    • Parmesan
  • Seasonings:
    • Dried parsley
    • salt
    • black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Water: Pour 1/2 cup of water around the edges of the baking dish before baking.
