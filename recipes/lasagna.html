Lasagna Recipe

Description

A simple lasagna recipe that will save you lots of time and satisfy everyone at the dinner table.

Ingredients

  • Your choice of Meat:
    • 1 pound of lean Ground Beef
    • 1 pound of Turkey
    • 1 pound of Ham
  • Spaghetti Sauce: Use 1 (32oz) store-bought jar, homemade makes this a 'not-so-simple' recipe.
  • Cheeses:
    • 32oz of Cottage Cheese
    • 3 cups of shredded Mozzarella, divided
    • 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan
  • 2 Eggs
  • Seasonings:
    • 2tsp of Dried parsley
    • Salt to taste
    • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 9 Lasagna Noodles
  • Water: Pour 1/2 cup of water around the edges of the baking dish before baking.

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 350o F (175o C).
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high (5-7) heat. Cook and stir ground beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 8-10 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Stir in spaghetti sauce and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Combine cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, eggs, 1/2 of the grated parmesan, dried parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  4. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce in a 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, one 3/4 cups of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce; repeat layers once more. Top with remaining 3 noodles, sauce, mozzarella, and parmesan. Pour 1/2 cup water along the edges of the dish. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Let stand for 10 more minutes before serving.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
