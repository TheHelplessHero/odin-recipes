Pumpkin Mini-Cake

Description

A delicious pumpkin dessert to enhance your Thanksgiving meal or add some pumpkin spice any day of the week!

Ingredients

(Serving for 1)
  • Crust:
    • 1 (15.25oz) package yellow cake mix, divided
    • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
    • 1 large egg
  • Filling:
    • 1 (29oz) can pumpkin puree
    • 3 large eggs
    • 2/3 cup milk
    • 1/2 cup brown sugar
    • 2tbsp pumpkin pie spice
  • Topping:
    • 1/2 cup white sugar
    • 1/4 cup cold unsalted butter, cubed
    • 3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 350o F (175o C). Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Make crust:
    • Transfer 1 cup cake mix to a small bowl; set aside for topping. Place remaining cake mix in a medium bowl iwth melted butter and egg; mix until well blended.
  4. Spread mixture into the bottom of the prepared baking dish.
  5. Making filling:
    • Combine pumpkin puree, eggs, milk, brown sugar, and pumpkin pie spice in a large bowl; mix well and pour over crust.
  6. Make topping:
    • Add white sugar and cubed butter to reserved cake mix; use a pastry blender to mix until mixture resembles caorse crumbs.
  7. Sprinkle over pumpkin filling, then scatter chopped walnuts over the top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until the top is a golden brown, roughly 45-50 minutes.
